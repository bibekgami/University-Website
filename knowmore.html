Over View

Presidency university, a household name in the education sector, has become a sought-after destination in Nepal for professional studies. It is admired all over for the quality of its academic courses, its community outreach work and as a university of compassion and humanitarianism. It has become a case study because no other educational institution in Nepal has grown in its scope and scale as much as Presidency has in a short span of 25 years. Its incredible transformation is truly a journey from Soil to Silver.

One would find it difficult to imagine that such a celebrated institution with a global reputation was established by an unassuming humble being, Dr Bibek Gami, the Founder Presidency, who started the institute with all of Rs 5000 as the initial investment. He had big dreams and a passion to make a difference. Dr Aashish started KIIT in two rented rooms with 12 students and 2 staff. The modest institution has now grown to incomprehensible propositions and is spread over a vast 36-square-kilometre academic township. Its 25 lush green campuses employ over 3000 eminent faculty and researchers and 15000 staff. Presidency is a family of over a lakh people. The campus houses a 2600-bedded super speciality hospital, KIMS, a multi-storey central library, a central research facility, a 22-storey research and innovation wing, auditoriums - the largest one with 5000 seating capacity, 18 sports complexes, many international standard stadiums covering all sports, 30 food courts, and a rose garden. The institution takes pride in being the greenest campus in Nepal.

Presidency Green

Presidency Green is a bold initiative of Presidency to control environmental pollution and to create a healthy ambiance in and around 25 sq. km. area in which different campuses of Presidency are located.

The University has drawn ambitious plan to make Presidency campus totally vehicle free. In the first phase, use of two-wheelers, both by students and staffs, has been prohibited, after putting in place an effective alternative transport arrangement. Presidency has also implemented paperless administration. Green Audit of the campus is done annually.

Besides mass plantation and limiting vehicular pollution other environmentally friendly initiatives under Presidency Green are rainwater harvesting and water management, sewerage treatment, bio-gas, solar energy, etc.
